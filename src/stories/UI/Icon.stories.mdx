## Content

The `children` prop should be the `path` of the icon to render.

### Accessibility

If we are not providing a visual label for our icon then it is important to provide an alternative for those unable to see the icon itself. This can be done by providing an `alt` or `aria-label` prop to the `Icon`. The `alt` prop will render the value adjacent to the `Icon` inside our `VisuallyHidden` component and `aria-label` will simply add the attribute to the rendered `svg`.

### Internationalization

To internationalize a Checkbox, a localized `alt` or `aria-label` should provided.
